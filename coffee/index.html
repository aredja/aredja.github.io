Milchbar

Zurich, Switzerland

7.8 / 10
Coffee
8
Atmosphere
9
Service
7

The espresso can be rather sour. The cappuccino is usually very good, unless it spends ten minutes waiting at the bar before being served. The fountain gives the courtyard a particularly pleasant atmosphere, and on weekdays it is a very good place to meet friends.

Personal bonus: this is where I met my wife.
