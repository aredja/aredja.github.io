Thinking in a café is quite hard, if the only thing you are thinking about is how terrible your coffee tastes. This personal coffee atlas is inspired by [Ariel Rubinstein’s Coffee Atlas](https://cafeatlas.org/). I am no coffee expert, but I do enjoy rambling about what a very good cappuccino or espresso should look and taste like. Unless stated otherwise, I always order a cappucino.
All ratings are, of course, entirely subjective. They may depend on the barista, the exact time of the visit, and my personal mood at the time. Alongside coffee, atmosphere, and service, I also rate each café’s suitability for thinking up your next research idea, only to discard it the following morning.
